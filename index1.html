Welcome to E-Khata

Electronic Khata (E-Khata) is a digital ledger system that allows businesses to keep track of their transactions electronically. It offers several advantages over traditional physical khata books, including:

  • Convenience: Accessible from anywhere with an internet connection.
  • Accuracy: Minimizes human error in recording transactions.
  • Backup and Security: Data is securely stored and easily backed up.
  • Efficiency: Streamlines the process of managing accounts and generating reports.

E-Khata solves the problem of physical khata by providing a digital solution that is more efficient, accurate, and accessible.
